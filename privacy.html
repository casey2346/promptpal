Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

PromptPal is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Storage: Your prompts and API keys are stored locally in your browser only
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand website usage (anonymous data)
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, or personal information

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our service
  • To understand how users interact with our platform
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

Data Storage

All your prompts and API keys are stored locally in your browser. We do not have access to this information, and it is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements
  • AI APIs: Your prompts are sent directly to AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) using your API keys

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
